## 3. Scope of Cooperation

3.1 **Member contribution.** Member contributes [SPECIFIC CONTRIBUTION
DESCRIPTION] into the W3A federation, on the integration architecture
documented separately at [INTEGRATION BRIEF REFERENCE].

3.2 **W3A access.** W3A grants Member access to:

- The licensed substrate at the executed Tier-2 (LEL) / Tier-3
  (LRL-PR) / SCLA terms.
- The cross-Member routing layer and distribution surface.
- The W3A capital pool at the documented Layer-2 terms.
- The Member-Coordination Forum (quarterly cadence).
- Standard contractual representations and warranties on substrate
  title, W3A IP, and partner-ecosystem performance.

3.3 **Brand-and-IP discipline.** Member operates the W3A surface under
the brand-via-env-override pattern. No Member's brand displaces W3A in
alliance-surface chrome. Member's own brand displaces W3A in Member's
own customer-facing surfaces.

3.4 **Clean-room provenance.** Member acknowledges the Clean-Room
Engineering Protocol (`docs.w3a.foundation/clean-room`) and agrees to
respect its substance for any work that extends substrate modules.

---

## 4. Commercial Structure

4.1 **Revenue split.** The Parties share **fifty percent (50%) / fifty
percent (50%)** of **Net Partnership Revenue** above Documented Costs.

4.2 **Documented Costs.** Each Party maintains, and shares quarterly
with the other, a documented record of its costs of operating the
Joint Surface.

4.3 **Settlement cadence.** Net Partnership Revenue is computed
monthly; settlement within 30 days of month-end.

4.4 **Pricing of customer-facing services.** Customer-facing fees are
jointly set by the Parties consistent with regulatory constraints and
the W3A fee policy.

4.5 **Cross-routing.** Either Party may route additional flow to the
Joint Surface from its own customer relationships; revenue on routed
flow is allocated per §4.1 unless the routing Party and the receiving
Party agree in writing otherwise.

---

## 5. Term and Termination

5.1 **Initial Term.** Three (3) years from Effective Date.

5.2 **Renewal.** Automatic renewal for successive one (1)-year periods
unless either Party gives written notice of non-renewal no less than
sixty (60) days before the end of the then-current period.

5.3 **Termination for cause.** Either Party may terminate immediately
on written notice if the other Party (a) materially breaches and fails
to cure within thirty (30) days; (b) becomes insolvent or files for
bankruptcy; (c) loses regulatory authorisation material to its
performance.

5.4 **Effect of termination.** Each Party retains its pre-existing IP;
in-flight customer transactions are completed; books-and-records
retention obligations survive per applicable law; Net Partnership
Revenue is reconciled and finally settled; cooperation in customer-
migration is required for a reasonable wind-down period.

---

## 6. Intellectual Property

6.1 **W3A pre-existing IP.** W3A and the Coordination Layer retain all
right, title, and interest in the substrate, brand registry, and
Alliance-owned patents.

6.2 **Member pre-existing IP.** Member retains all right, title, and
interest in its own pre-existing IP.

6.3 **Adapter / integration code.** The Member-side integration
adapter, branded surface, and any other code that bridges the W3A
substrate to Member's own systems is Member IP and licensed to W3A
under the LEL on terms no less favourable than W3A's standard
licensing posture for Members.

6.4 **No transfer of source.** Neither Party transfers source code to
the other beyond the licence grants of §6.1–6.3.
